Quick Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

📦 What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our MOQ starts at just 500 pieces — making us ideal for startups, small businesses, and first-time buyers. Larger bulk orders get better per-piece pricing.
🧾 Do you provide a GST invoice?
Yes, 100%. A proper GST invoice is issued for every order — making your purchase fully tax-compliant and claimable for input tax credit (ITC).
🎁 Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?
Absolutely. We dispatch free samples for evaluation before you commit to bulk production. Just fill the enquiry form and request samples in the specifications field.
🚚 How long does delivery take?
Standard orders are delivered in 7–15 working days from design approval. Express orders may be available for urgent requirements — ask us when enquiring.
🎨 What sizes, GSM, and colours are available?
We offer fully custom sizes, GSM ranging from 40 to 120, and 20+ standard colours with Pantone matching available for brand-accurate printing.
💰 Is advance payment required to place an order?
50% advance payment is required to confirm your order. The remaining 50% is due after the GST bill is generated and before dispatch. We also provide complete packaging videos of your order so you can verify quality before delivery.
